about

I’m a postgraduate student at the University of Oxford pursuing a joint M.Sc. degree between the Mathematical Institute and Department of Computer Science at the University of Oxford as a member of Exeter College. Before pursuing this degree I did undergraduate at Purdue University and a spent a couple of years at MongoDB.

mongodb
